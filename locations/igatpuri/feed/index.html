Idyllic Location and Mesmerizing Views:
Ukiyo-569 is located at an elevation of 2000 ft. above sea level, offering breathtaking views of the lake and valleys. Its serene and picturesque setting provides a perfect escape from the routine lifestyle of the city.
The L-shaped infinity pool with a pool deck allows you to immerse yourself in the beauty of the surroundings while enjoying a refreshing swim or lounging in the sun. The pool area offers stunning views of the lake and the soaring hills beyond, creating an atmosphere of tranquility and relaxation.

Contemporary Interiors and Lavish Amenities:
The villa features contemporary interiors adorned with elegant sit-outs, open swings, and spacious recreational spaces. The linear structure of the home ensures that you can enjoy the wafting breeze and uninterrupted water views from any corner.
Each of the five spacious bedrooms is designed as an independent unit, providing secluded privacy. The rooms are tastefully decorated with distinct paintings, adding character to the space. Amenities such as AC, TV, and attached furnished patios ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay.
The ensuite bathrooms are spacious and equipped with lavish amenities. The ground-floor bathrooms also feature lake-facing bathtubs, allowing you to indulge in a luxurious bathing experience while enjoying the beautiful vistas.

Outdoor Bliss and Spectacular Views:
The villa is set on 2.5 acres of meticulously landscaped farmland, offering ample space to unwind and relax. Scattered throughout the property are spacious nooks, open swings, and comfortable seating areas that face the mesmerizing lake view, providing perfect spots to soak in the natural beauty.
Guests can witness the captivating views of the sunrise and sunset, creating magical moments during their stay. The changing hues of the sky and the shimmering lake create an ethereal ambiance that is sure to leave a lasting impression.

Recreational Activities and Proximity to Nature:
Ukiyo-569 offers a curated collection of indoor games, ensuring that guests of all ages can stay entertained. Whether you prefer a friendly game of carrom, snooker, or cards, there is something for everyone.

Thoughtful Amenities for a Comfortable Stay:
The villa is equipped with essential amenities to ensure your absolute comfort. Each bedroom has a private entrance, and amenities such as AC, Wi-Fi, wardrobe, and hangers are provided.
Additional amenities include an iron, torch, washing machine, central heater, towels, toiletries, a medical kit, and mosquito repellent. The villa has parking space for up to six cars and five bikes, ensuring convenience for guests with their vehicles.

At Ukiyo-569, you can immerse yourself in the serene ambiance, unwind by the infinity pool, and enjoy the breathtaking lake views. Indulge in the comfort of contemporary interiors, explore the outdoor spaces, and create unforgettable memories amidst the natural beauty that surrounds you. Whether you seek relaxation, recreational activities, or a connection with nature, Ukiyo-569 promises a rejuvenating holiday in your own private oasis.
