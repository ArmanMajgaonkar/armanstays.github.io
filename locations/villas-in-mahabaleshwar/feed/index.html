This artistically decorated 5-bedroom villa in Mahabaleshwar spans across two storeys, featuring a beautiful lawn and a garden adorned with vibrant and colorful flowers. On the ground floor, you will find three bedrooms, each equipped with its own ensuite bathroom. Moving to the first storey, there are two more bedrooms, also with ensuite bathrooms. The upper floor additionally boasts a spacious living room, a dining room, a fully equipped kitchen, and a large balcony, providing ample space for relaxation and entertainment.

Ukiyo-522 is an ideal destination for serene retreats with family and friends. Couples seeking a romantic getaway will find solace in the tranquility and beauty that this villa and its surroundings offer.
