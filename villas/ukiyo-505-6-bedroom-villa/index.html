Overview

Item Code: 13-26104
₹40000
Escape the chaos of city life and embark on a tranquil retreat at Ukiyo-505. This picturesque villa is nestled amidst breathtaking valley vistas, offering a serene getaway for you, your friends, and family. Immerse yourself in a unique experience that feels straight out of a movie, with a sprawling lush green garden and a stunning fountain. Don’t miss the opportunity to visit the glass observatory for a night of uninterrupted stargazing.

Home Amenities:
Generator, AC, heater, and Wi-Fi are provided.
Wardrobe, hangers, iron, torch, baby bassinet, washing machine, and dryer are available.
Geysers, towels, and toiletries are provided in the bathrooms.
A medical kit and mosquito repellent are available.
Enjoy a barbecue meal during your stay.
A secured parking space is available for up to 10 cars.
This property is pet-friendly.

The Space:
Bedrooms:
There are 6 air-conditioned bedrooms: 2 on the ground floor, 3 on the first floor, and 1 in the basement.
All rooms offer a king-sized bed, Wi-Fi, electric kettle, attached bathrooms, and garden/hill views.
One of the bedrooms also features a heater and an attached balcony.

Bathrooms:
There are 6 attached bathrooms and 2 common bathrooms: one on the ground floor and one in the basement.
All attached bathrooms have a separate dressing area, geysers, towels, and basic toiletries.

Common Areas:
The villa features 2 spacious living rooms: one on the ground floor and one in the basement.
The ground floor living room offers sofa-style seating for up to 8 people, equipped with a wired music system, TV, and CCTV camera for 24/7 security.
The basement living room can accommodate up to 10 people, equipped with a wired music system, TV, and CCTV camera for 24/7 security.
Please note that the living rooms are not equipped with AC.
The dining area is part of the ground floor living room, with a rectangular dining table that can seat up to 10 people.
The villa’s exteriors feature a sprawling 7,500 sq. ft. garden with a water fountain.
The glasshouse offers stunning views of the valleys and mountains, providing a perfect spot for relaxation.

Kitchen:
Guests do not have access to the kitchen premises.
A water purifier, crockery, and cutlery are provided.

Food:
Barbecue meals can be arranged at an additional cost of Rs. 999/- per person (Veg/Chicken), including Veg/Egg/Chicken Biryani.
Regular meals can be made available at an additional cost of Rs. 1600/- per day per person for chicken/egg meals, and Rs. 1800/- per day per person for mutton/fish meals.
Any additional items ordered will be charged separately.
Non-vegetarian food consumption is allowed.

Guest Access:
You will have access to the entire villa. However, due to the COVID-19 pandemic, some shared amenities may have restricted access.

Interaction with Guests:
The host is not present at the property but can be reached via call or message. A trained and responsible caretaker will be available to address any concerns and ensure you have a perfect stay.

Staff Accommodation:
Accommodation for 4 female staff members can be provided at an additional cost of Rs. 2000 per person, per day (excluding meal charges). The staff will be accommodated in-house.

Activities:
Within the property, guests can enjoy indoor games like cards and ludo. Take a peaceful stroll in the glasshouse and savor the blissful views. Relish a lavish barbecue spread and request a bonfire to be lit up.

Add-On Services:
Barbecue meals can be arranged at an additional cost.
The washing machine can be used at no additional cost.
Bonfire can be arranged at no additional cost.
Prices may vary based on availability and peak season rates.

Home Truths:
To ensure there are no unpleasant surprises during your stay, here are a few important points to note:
This is a pet-friendly property.
The living rooms are not equipped with AC.
The location of the villa is secluded.
CCTV cameras capture the living rooms and external areas for 24/7 security.
In case of a power outage, the generator can support all the appliances for up to 24 hours.
The caretaker resides on the premises and is available from 08:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Additional services from the caretaker will be charged separately.
A baby bassinet can be provided upon request.
The home theater room is accessed from the outside and is not connected to the villa.
Complimentary Wi-Fi is available, subject to network availability.
All mobile networks work fairly well in the area.
Due to the monsoon season, there may be water seepage in the villas.

Pet Policy:
Pets are welcome, but only up to 1 pet is allowed.
Pets are not permitted on beds, futons, chairs, couches, or blankets.
Please clean up after your pets both inside the villa and in the garden area.
Pets are not allowed inside the kitchen to prevent pet hair from falling into the food.
Please bring your pet’s favorite blanket, as the towels/blankets at the villa are not accessible to them.
Groom your pets prior to arrival, especially if they are prone to shedding, as pet hair can be difficult to remove.
A refundable deposit will be collected before check-in if a pet accompanies the guests.

Neighborhood:
Panchgani, originally discovered as a summer resort, is now a popular hill station known for its breathtaking sunrise and sunset points. Surrounded by the Sahyadri hills, it offers a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. While enjoying your luxurious stay at Ukiyo-505, here are a few nearby places and activities we recommend:
Explore the enchanting flora at Kaas Plateau, a UNESCO World Heritage site.
Immerse yourself in art and creativity at Devrai Art Village.
Experience the lush carpet of grass at Table Land.

Getting Around:
Pune Airport: 150 km.
Satara Railway Station: 57 km.
Panchgani Bus Stand: 9 km.
Bhilar Market: 2 km.
Mumbai Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport: 249 km.
Mapro Garden: 7.2 km.
