]]> This holiday home, named Ukiyo 427, is a stunning property that offers an unparalleled earthy charm. It is nestled amidst nature, away from the city’s prying eyes, making it an ideal nature escape. The brown wooden facade and cosy indoor spaces complement each other beautifully, and the villa boasts modern, wooden interiors. The property features a private swimming pool that overlooks the green surroundings and a living room perfect for reading. The designated barbecue area adds to the experience.

The property’s key features are its serene location amidst green verdancy, private plunge pool, and overlooks of gorgeous landscapes. For your comfort, the villa provides a generator, AC, Wi-Fi, barbecue kit, wardrobe, hangers, torch, geysers, towels, toiletries, medical kit, mosquito repellent, and secured parking space for up to 3 cars. However, please note that this is not a wheelchair-friendly or pet-friendly property.

The villa has three cosy bedrooms, all located on the first floor, with amenities such as king-sized beds, Wi-Fi access, and ACs. Two bedrooms have attached bathrooms. In total, there are two attached bathrooms and one common bathroom, all with geysers, towels, and basic toiletries.

The first floor of the villa has a spacious living room adorned with comfortable furnishings for up to eight people, a TV, and Wi-Fi. The dining area can seat up to nine people. Guests can relax in the private swimming pool, measuring 15×12 ft. and 4.5 ft. in depth, and enjoy the serene surroundings. The basement features a barbecue area, and guests can also take a walk on the walking path or use the designated kids’ play area. A temple is also located on the premises.

Guests do not have access to the kitchen to prepare their own meals. Instead, guests may ask the caretaker’s assistance, and ingredients will be charged as per actuals for the same. The kitchen is equipped with a microwave, gas stove, refrigerator, water purifier, toaster, and mixer-grinder. Crockery and cutlery are also available.

The guests have access to the entire villa, except for the kitchen. However, due to the COVID-19 pandemic, guests may have restricted access to any shared amenities. The host is not present at the property, but a trained and responsible caretaker is present to resolve any concerns and help guests have a perfect stay. Unfortunately, the property cannot provide accommodation for personal staff.

Guests can enjoy a refreshing splash in the swimming pool and savour the tranquil surroundings within the property. They can play indoor games like cards and ludo or enjoy a barbecue meal while their kids play in the kids’ play area. Guests can also take a soothing walk in the mornings or go birdwatching and admire the Kingfisher and other bird species that are seldom spotted here.

The property is located in Malavali, near Lonavala, an escapist hill station surrounded by green valleys and waterfalls. Lonavala is one of the most sought-after tourist getaways near Mumbai and Pune, offering scenic lakes, waterfalls, dams, cliff tops, eluding caves, and forts.. So, while you are enjoying your stay at this property, here are a few nearby places and activities we recommend to make your stay even more memorable.
– Trek to the nearby Fort Visapur and Lohagad Fort
– Visit the Karla Caves and Bhaja Caves
– Seek blessings at Ekvira Devi Temple
– Head over to Bushi dam to have a relaxing time
– Experience the thrill of the theme park in Imagica
– Have some pictures clicked at the celebrity wax museum
– Enjoy a serene view from Tiger or Sunset Point

Before booking, please note that guests are advised to remain cautious due to the presence of snakes, insects, bugs, crabs, and reptiles around. A friendly dog resides on the property, and guests do not have access to the storeroom, staff room, and a few lockers. All-male groups are not allowed, and please be mindful and keep noise to a minimum after 10 PM. There is a CCTV camera for security purposes. Additionally, guests cannot hire or bring any outside vendors for any events at the property. Guests can stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi. Network is subject to availability at any given time.Mobile networks such as Jio, Airtel, Vi work fairly well here.

Lonavala is located at a distance of 70 km from Pune Airport, 10 km from Lonavala Railway Station and Bus Stand, and 11 km from the Lonavala Market.
